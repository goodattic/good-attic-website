Common attic problems

What homeowners in St. Louis usually notice first.

These are the attic symptoms that tend to start the conversation before homeowners know exactly which service they need.

Hot, humid upper floors

Mixed-humid summers put attic heat and upstairs comfort on full display in many St. Louis homes.

Cold-weather heat loss

Winter drafts and weak attic performance can make older homes harder to keep comfortable.

Musty or aging insulation

Older attics can collect dust, moisture history, and material that is no longer worth building on.

Pest contamination

Squirrels, mice, and other attic pests can turn a comfort problem into a cleanup project quickly.

FAQ

Questions homeowners ask in St. Louis.

Why is my second floor so hot in summer?

That is one of the most common attic-related complaints in St. Louis. Heat buildup, weak insulation, and air leakage often all play a role.

Does attic work help in winter too?

Yes. The same attic that drives summer discomfort can also be responsible for winter drafts and heat loss.

Can you help after rodents or squirrels have been in the attic?

Yes. Attic pest remediation, removal, sanitation, and rebuild work are all part of the Good Attic system.
