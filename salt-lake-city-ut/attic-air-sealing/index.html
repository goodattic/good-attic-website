FAQ

Questions about attic air sealing in Salt Lake City.

Does attic air sealing still matter if I already have insulation?

Yes. Insulation and sealing do different jobs, and many comfort complaints involve both.

What kinds of openings get sealed?

Common targets include pipe penetrations, wiring openings, top-plate gaps, fixture penetrations, and other bypasses between the house and attic.

Can air sealing help with dust or odors too?

It can. Closing attic leakage paths can reduce how easily dusty or stale attic air moves into the living space.

Does attic air sealing in Salt Lake City still matter if the home already has insulation?

Yes. Insulation and air sealing do different jobs. Many homes in Salt Lake City have insulation present but still leak enough at the attic floor to lose comfort and efficiency.

What usually tells you attic air sealing in Salt Lake City should be part of the project?

We usually see it when the attic has obvious bypasses, dusty air movement, uneven comfort, or insulation that cannot perform well because the boundary underneath it is still open.
